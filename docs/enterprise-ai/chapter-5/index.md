<h1>Chapter 05 — Agents and Tool Use</h1>

<p class="hero-subtitle">
Understanding how AI systems move beyond answering questions to planning, reasoning, using tools, and executing real-world tasks.
</p>

<p class="hero-description">
Large Language Models can generate knowledge, but agents extend those capabilities by interacting with tools, accessing memory, coordinating workflows, and pursuing objectives. This chapter explores the emerging world of agentic AI and the architectures that enable intelligent systems to participate in work rather than simply describe it.
</p>

</div>
