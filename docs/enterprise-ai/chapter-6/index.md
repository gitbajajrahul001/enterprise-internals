<h1>Chapter 06 — AI Infrastructure & Operations</h1>

<p class="hero-subtitle">
Understanding the infrastructure, platforms, operations, and economics that power modern AI systems at scale.
</p>

<p class="hero-description">
AI is not just a model problem. Behind every AI application sits a complex platform composed of GPUs, storage systems, networking, inference platforms, observability tooling, and operational processes. This chapter explores the infrastructure layer that enables modern AI systems to train, serve, scale, and operate reliably in enterprise environments.
</p>

</div>
