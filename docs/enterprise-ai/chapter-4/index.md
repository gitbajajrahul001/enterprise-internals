<h1>Chapter 04 — Building AI Systems</h1>

<p class="hero-subtitle">
Understanding how modern AI applications are designed, assembled, governed, and operated in production environments.
</p>

<p class="hero-description">
Models alone do not create AI solutions. Modern AI systems combine prompts, context, retrieval, grounding, caching, gateways, observability, and governance into complete architectures that can reliably deliver intelligence at scale. This chapter explores how these components work together to form enterprise-grade AI platforms.
</p>

</div>
