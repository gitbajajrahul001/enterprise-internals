<h1>Chapter 02 — Data & Knowledge Architecture</h1>

<p class="hero-subtitle">
Understanding how modern AI systems store, represent, retrieve, and utilize knowledge.
</p>

<p class="hero-description">
Large Language Models are only one part of an AI system. The real challenge lies in how information is organized, retrieved, grounded, and delivered to those models. This chapter explores the foundations of enterprise knowledge architecture that power modern AI applications.
</p>
